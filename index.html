Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need inventory or a warehouse?
Nope! That's the beauty of print-on-demand. Products are only created when someone orders them. No storage, no unsold inventory, no risk.
How long does fulfillment take?
Most products are printed and ready to ship within 2-7 business days. Shipping time depends on the destination, but typically adds another 3-5 business days for domestic orders.
What about shipping costs?
Printify handles all shipping logistics and provides tracking information automatically. Shipping costs are calculated based on product weight, size, and destination. I'll include shipping estimates in your quote.
Will I know when my order is fulfilled?
Yes! Printify provides automatic updates throughout the production and shipping process. You'll receive notifications when your order enters production, when it ships, and tracking information to follow its journey.
What if I need help with my design or logo?
No problem! If your logo needs adjustment for printing, I can help with basic formatting. Just send me what you have, and we'll make sure it looks great on your products.
Can I order just one item to test it out?
Absolutely! There are no minimum orders. Order a single mug or t-shirt to see the quality before committing to larger quantities.
What products can you create?
Mugs, t-shirts, hoodies, hats, glassware, tote bags, stickers, posters, and much more. If you have something specific in mind, just ask! Printify has hundreds of products available.
How much does this cost?
Pricing depends on the product type, quantity, and customization. Generally, products range from $8-$25 per item for production, plus shipping. I'll provide a detailed quote before you commit to anything.
Can you ship directly to my customers?
Yes! If you want to sell merch online or offer it as rewards, I can arrange for orders to ship directly to your customers with your branding. Perfect for e-commerce stores or customer appreciation gifts.
What payment methods do you accept?
I use Stripe for secure payment processing, which accepts all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets. Safe, fast, and easy.

Still have questions?
