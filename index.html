Frequently Asked Questions

How does shipping work?

We ship directly to your business or your customer's door. Shipping costs are calculated based on weight and destination.

What kind of files do I need for my logo?

High-resolution PNG or JPG files work best. Vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) are even better for maintaining crisp quality.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Absolutely. Once you place an order, we'll send a digital mockup for your approval before anything goes to production.
